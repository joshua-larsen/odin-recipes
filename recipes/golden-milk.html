Golden Milk


Franz Eugen Köhler, Köhler's Medizinal-Pflanzen, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Golden milk is made of various herbs and spices as well as a milk of your choice. The most important ingredients is turmeric which gives the milk a golden colour.

Ingredients

    5 ml Turmeric Powder

    5 ml Cinnamon Powder

    5 ml Ginger Powder

    5 ml Black Pepper

    5 ml Sweetner

    250 ml Milk of your choice

Instructions

    Add ingredients together into a glass.

    Stir until all ingredients are mixed together.

    Heat in microwave for 1 minute.

    Stir again.

    Enjoy!
