Miso Soup


https://publicdomainq.net/miso-0018849/, CC0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Miso soup is a traditional dish throughout Japan typically made of a fermented soy paste (miso) in addition to bonito flakes and various vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 250ml Water
  • 20g Miso paste of your choosing
  • 5g Dried seaweed such as wakame
  • 50g Tofu
  • 1 Spring onion

Instructions

  1. Bring water to a boil in a small saucepan.
  2. Soak dried seaweed in water for 5 minutes.
  3. Chop spring onion and tofu into small pieces.
  4. Turn off heat and add miso paste, seaweed, spring onion and tofu.
  5. Stir until miso paste is dissolved.
  6. Enjoy!
